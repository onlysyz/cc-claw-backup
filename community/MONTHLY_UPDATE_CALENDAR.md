## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### YouTube
- Upload Tuesday or Wednesday
- 5-10 minutes for tutorials
- Eye-catching thumbnail
- First 30 seconds critical

### Dev.to
- Post Thursday or Friday
- Use relevant tags
- Include code snippets
- Add canonical link to your blog

### Twitter
- Post Tuesday-Thursday
- Images get 2x engagement
- Threads > single tweets
- Engage in first hour

### Discord
- Monday for announcements
- Friday for community vibes
- Always pin important posts

### LinkedIn
- Post Wednesday or Thursday
- Professional tone
- Articles > posts for long content
