## Viral Loop Strategy

```
1. Developer stars repo
        ↓
2. Gets "thanks for starring" email with:
   - Quick start link
   - Share badge for their followers
        ↓
3. Developer shares "I use cc-claw"
        ↓
4. Their followers check it out
        ↓
5. Some star, some buy PRO
        ↓
6. Repeat with network effects
```

1. **Get 50+ stars in 24 hours** (main requirement)
2. **Post on social media** when you see it trending
3. **Contact friends** to star quickly
4. **Post in communities** simultaneously

### Trending Timeline

```
Day 1 Morning:
- Post Twitter: "cc-claw just launched"
```
