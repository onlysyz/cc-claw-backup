## Best Practices

### Timing
- Twitter DMs: 9 AM - 11 AM PST on Tue-Thu
- Follow up: 3 days after no response
- Max 2 follow-ups per person

### Personalization
- Reference specific tweet/post they made
- Mention something you agree/disagree with
- Show you actually use their content

### What Works
- Genuine admiration for their work
- Clear value proposition
- Low-friction next step (DM, form link)
- Flexibility on collaboration format

### What Doesn't Work
- Generic mass DMs
- Asking for free promotion
- Overly formal/business language
- Multiple attachments in first DM
