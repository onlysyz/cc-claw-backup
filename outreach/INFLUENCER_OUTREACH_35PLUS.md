## Best Practices

### Timing (Optimal)
- Twitter DMs: 9 AM - 11 AM PST on Tue-Thu
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Avoid: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Personalization Checklist
- [ ] Reference a specific tweet/post
- [ ] Mention shared interest/alignment
- [ ] Customize the value prop for their audience
- [ ] Keep it under 280 characters if possible
- [ ] Include clear CTA

### What Works
- Genuine compliment on their work
- Clear value prop in first line
- Low-friction next step
- Flexibility on format

### What Doesn't Work
- Generic mass DMs
- Asking for immediate promotion
- Overly formal language
- Multiple attachments
- Wall of text
